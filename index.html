Bellabeat Capstone Project

As part of our final assignemnt for the Google Data Analytics Program, we were given 2 data sets to chose from and analyze, which I chose BellaBeat. This is an app designed to help women monitor their health and achieve their fitness goals. We were tasked with analyzing data from a competing fitness tracking company, Fitbit, and identify trends to help guide BellaBeats marketing strategies. In this analysis, I utilized R Studio to both clean, analyize, and visualize my findings and interpret the data.
